Lerveno Veno is the founder and CEO of Lerveno Tech. He is an Italian entrepreneur with a background in automotive design, having worked for a luxury car brand before venturing into the technology industry. Lerveno's passion for innovation and design led him to create Lerveno Tech, a company that combines timeless Italian craftsmanship with modular innovation to produce enduring devices that eliminate digital waste and restore reliability to everyday life. Under his leadership, Lerveno Tech has become a global technical organization known for crafting technology that is not disposable but designed to evolve with its owners.
