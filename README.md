## Project Overview
Lerveno technology 
Lerveno technology is a business created in early 2026. The business is named after the owner, Lerveno Veno, in Milan, Italy. The owner was an automotive designer for a luxury car brand before embarking on the world of technology. 
His first product was the home control system [HCS]. It is an ai controlled home assistant, which he then named Lerveno. This idea made him successful enough for him to move out of his studio in Milan into a bigger place in Italy. He then later started creating gadgets for everyday use, so with his experience with design and luxury, he found it fitting to combine both to create this business, which provides technology with a little touch of elegance.  
 Our vision and mission. We exist to redefine the bond between humans and technology by merging timeless Italian craftsmanship with modular innovation — creating enduring devices that eliminate digital waste and restore reliability to everyday life. As a global technical organization, we strive for excellence in every aspect of our work, crafting technology that is not disposable but designed to evolve with its owners — reliable heirlooms built to last and inspire trust for generations.
